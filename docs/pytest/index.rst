
Pytest Integration
==================

`Pytest <https://docs.pytest.org/en/latest/>`_ is one of the most mature and widely used testing
frameworks when it comes to Python.

It comes with plenty of plugins that make it highly customizable.
This is why Pykiso can also be integrated within `pytest <https://docs.pytest.org/en/latest/>`_ as a plugin.
